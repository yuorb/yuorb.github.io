8.0 Adjuncts

Adjuncts were introduced in Sec. 2.5. They are placed adjacent to a formative to provide further grammatical or semantic information about the formative. There are seven types of Adjuncts: Affixual, Modular, Register, Suppletive, Mood/Case-Scope,[1] Bias, and the Parsing Adjunct, each explained in the sub-sections below.
